The default settings for the layer's configuration are provided by the layersConfig service. You may want to override some value, for instance the timestamp, the opacity or if the layer is queryable or not.

In this example, we choose to change the opacity of the background map to 0.4, and display the aerial photography of the year 1954 only (by default you would have all available year from 1924 to today).
